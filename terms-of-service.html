1️⃣ Nature of the Service

ZR Partner is a server-partnership management system that operates exclusively within the Discord servers on which it is installed. It lets designated "realizators" register partnerships (invite exchanges) with other servers, enforces an activity quota for realizators, and automatically ends partnerships when a partner leaves a server.

2️⃣ Realizator Norm (Activity Quota)

  • ⏱️ Members holding the realizator role must complete a set number of partnerships within each 2-day cycle.
  • 📩 A reminder is sent by DM after 1 day if the quota has not yet been met.
  • 🚫 If the quota is not met by the end of the cycle, the realizator role is automatically removed.
  • 🌴 The norm does not apply while a realizator holds an approved leave role; it resumes with a fresh cycle once leave ends.

3️⃣ Reward Points

  • 🎁 Once a realizator reaches a set number of completed partnerships, they may open a payout ticket. Server staff then manually credit the corresponding virtual points to that realizator's balance via the ZR Points bot — ZR Partner does not process or facilitate any monetary payment.
  • 🚫 Points have no monetary value and cannot be exchanged for real currency or assets outside the server.
  • 📊 The rate of points assigned per partnership may differ for a brand-new partnership versus a renewed one (the same partner/server pairing after the cooldown period).
  • ⚖️ Server staff reserve the right to correct point balances or partnership counts in the event of system errors, abuse, or exploits.
  • 📄 Use of awarded points is additionally governed by the ZR Points bot's own Terms of Service.

4️⃣ Partnership Rules

The following limits and restrictions apply to the partnership system:

  • 🔁 A maximum of 2 partnerships may be registered with the same partner within a 7-day period.
  • 🪞 A 2-day cooldown applies between partnerships registered by a realizator involving themselves.
  • 🚫 Servers appearing on the shared blacklist (maintained by a separate blacklist bot) cannot be partnered with.
  • 📎 Only valid Discord invite links (discord.gg / discord.com/invite) submitted in the designated channel are accepted for a partnership.

  • 🚫 Submitting fake, expired, or unauthorized invite links to register a partnership

  • 🚫 Trading reward points or partnership credit outside the mechanisms provided by the bot
Violation of the above rules may result in point balance corrections, removal of the realizator role, revocation of rewards, or other sanctions determined by the administration of the given server.
